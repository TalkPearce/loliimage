League OF Legends



Champion Picks



Diana

This is the most meta-changing pick I have, plus I am the most experienced on the champion. Usually the build is to go striaght Ap carry but recently I've been swapping out the gnashors tooth with a Guardian angel because you get as much basic attack damage but you also get armour and a powerful revive every five minuets. This can change the outcome of many early-game fights and is as strong as a Zhonha's Hourglass in many cases.



Volibear


The Volibear runes are exactly what's needed to bolster his macho fighting style. The build is carry style, but unfortunately is not working due to the fact you need 3 items and will only work if i snowball into a scaling position. This seems too ambitious as a regular carry and we should be looking to jungle carry the early game. He might be suitable as a carry top laner since I do not need to be battle heavy and i can control waves easily with the passive Lightning Strikes



K'Sante

K'Sante feels like a tricky top pick but I have been impressed with performances of other players using this champion. The best idea is to get the rhythm of his play-style and to start using him as a juggernaught. Hopefully this includes being as unkillable as on the champion Sett.
